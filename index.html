Hi! I am a Ph.D. candidate at University of Virginia majoring in Computer Science. I am advised by Prof. Aidong Zhang. My research interests are broadly in Machine Learning and Data Mining, with a focus on Multimodal Machine Learning, Graph Mining, Continual Learning, Federated Learning, and Efficient Knowledge Transfer.

Before joining UVa, I received my Bachelor’s degree in Automation Engineering and Master’s degree in Control Science and Engineering from Xi’an Jiaotong University. I worked at the Institute of Artificial Intelligence and Robotics during 2015-2018, under the supervision of Prof. Xuguang Lan and Prof. Nanning Zheng, focusing on projects related to Computer Vision, 3D Neural Style Transfer, and Robotics.

I also did summer internships at Google in 2022 and 2023, working on Multimodal Few-shot Learning, LLMs, and Model Compression.
